About Me

Hi, I’m Tou. I’m a UI and web designer/developer who enjoys making digital experiences feel clear, simple, and friendly to use.

I work on both design and development. I create layouts in Figma, then bring them to life with clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I like taking ideas that feel messy or confusing and turning them into interfaces that make sense.

I also specialize in using AI tools to enhance design, branding, and development workflows, helping me move faster while staying thoughtful and intentional.

Right now I’m freelancing, building websites, UI designs, and branding concepts for small businesses and personal projects. I care about thoughtful details, clear communication, and creating designs that actually work for real people.
